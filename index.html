🧠 How Adaptive Learning Works

📚 Spaced Repetition System

Words appear based on how well you know them. New words appear more frequently, while mastered words appear less often for long-term retention.

🎨 Color-Coded Learning States

New (N): Words you haven't learned yet
Learning (L): Words you're currently practicing (appear more often)
Review (R): Words you know but need periodic review
Mastered (M): Words you know well (appear rarely for maintenance)

🎯 Study Modes

📚 Study Mode: Traditional flashcards with adaptive word selection

🧠 Quiz Modes:

  • Multiple Choice: Standard 4-option quiz
  • Listening First: Hear pronunciation, then choose meaning
  • Mixed Challenge: Random Japanese→English or English→Japanese
  • Speed Challenge: 8-second time limit for quick recall

⏰ Review Timing

The system schedules reviews based on memory research: 30 minutes → 1 day → 3 days → 7 days → 14 days

💡 Study Tips

  • Study new words in Study Mode first, then test with quizzes
  • Use the audio button to learn proper pronunciation
  • Focus on words with due reviews (red alert) for best results
  • The system automatically prioritizes words you find difficult

🎮 Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Spacebar/Enter: Flip card or advance quiz
  • Arrow Keys: Navigate between cards
  • 1-4 Keys: Select quiz options

📊 Understanding Your Stats

  • Due Now: Words ready for review (focus here first!)
  • Learning Streak: Days since you first started studying
  • Mastery %: Percentage of words you've mastered
  • Category Progress: Visual breakdown by word type
