🧠 Complete Learning Guide

🎯 Quick Start Guide

New to the system? Start with Study Mode to learn new words, then use Quiz Modes to test your knowledge. The system will automatically schedule reviews based on how well you know each word.

📚 Spaced Repetition System

Words appear based on scientific memory research. New words show up frequently, while mastered words appear rarely for long-term retention. The system uses these intervals:

  • New words: Immediate practice
  • Learning: 30 minutes → 1 day
  • Review: 3 days → 7 days
  • Mastered: 14+ days

🎨 Learning State Colors

New (N): Words you haven't learned yet
Learning (L): Words you're actively practicing
Review (R): Words you know but need periodic review
Mastered (M): Words you know well (long-term memory)

📖 Study Mode

Traditional flashcards with smart word selection:

  • Click the card or press Space/Enter to flip
  • The system prioritizes words due for review
  • Each flip marks the word as "studied" and advances its learning state
  • Use audio button (🔊) to hear natural pronunciation

🧠 Quiz Modes

Multiple Choice: Standard 4-option quiz with enhanced distractors

Listening First: Hear pronunciation first, then choose meaning (great for developing listening skills)

Mixed Challenge: Random Japanese→English or English→Japanese with adaptive difficulty

Speed Challenge: 8-second time limit with extra-large kanji for quick recognition training

Kanji Only: Massive kanji display (72px) for pure character recognition practice

🎲 Enhanced Quiz Intelligence

  • Smart Distractors: Wrong answers are chosen from similar word types and meanings
  • Adaptive Difficulty: Questions become harder as you improve
  • Context Sensitivity: Mixed challenge adapts question format based on your learning patterns
  • Progressive Complexity: Kanji-only mode emphasizes visual recognition over reading

⏰ Optimal Study Strategy

Recommended Daily Routine:

  1. Check Due Reviews (Red Alert): Always start here for maximum retention
  2. Study New Words: 10-20 minutes in Study Mode
  3. Quiz Practice: 5-10 minutes testing what you studied
  4. Speed/Kanji Challenges: 5 minutes for automaticity training

🎮 Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Spacebar/Enter: Flip card or advance quiz
  • Arrow Keys: Navigate between cards
  • 1-4 Keys: Select quiz options quickly

📊 Understanding Your Progress

  • Due Now (Red): Words ready for review - prioritize these!
  • Learning Streak: Days since you started - consistency matters
  • Mastery Percentage: Overall progress toward N5 completion
  • Category Breakdown: Visual progress by word type (nouns, verbs, adjectives)
  • Upcoming Reviews: Plan your study schedule

🧪 Advanced Learning Science

The system implements research-backed techniques:

  • Interleaving: Mixed word types prevent rote memorization
  • Retrieval Practice: Active recall through quizzes
  • Desirable Difficulties: Challenging distractors improve retention
  • Distributed Practice: Spaced intervals optimize memory consolidation

💡 Pro Tips for JLPT N5 Success

  • Study consistently for 15-30 minutes daily rather than long cramming sessions
  • Use the audio feature extensively - pronunciation is crucial for the listening section
  • Focus on kanji recognition in Kanji Only mode - N5 has 80+ essential kanji
  • Practice speed challenges to improve automaticity for the real exam
  • Mix study and quiz modes within the same session for better retention
  • Don't ignore due reviews - they're scientifically scheduled for optimal memory strength

❓ Troubleshooting

  • Audio not working? Check internet connection - TTS requires online access
  • No due reviews? Study more words or wait for scheduled intervals
  • Quiz too easy/hard? The system adapts over time based on your performance
  • Lost progress? Data is saved locally - avoid clearing browser data

Remember: Language learning is a marathon, not a sprint. The spaced repetition system works best with consistent daily practice. Trust the process and stay consistent!
